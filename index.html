About US


At our academy, "Practice. Precision. Perfection." is more than just a motto—it’s the foundation of our training. My journey in table tennis began without a mentor, relying only on self-learning and determination. That experience drives me today to provide the structured guidance I once lacked. As a certified coach trained by SAI NIS, I am one of the very few in Tamil Nadu holding this elite certification, making our academy stand apart. Our program is designed to develop both the mind and body, ensuring that every athlete is always in peak form and capable of executing their game with precision.


We emphasize respect, discipline, and high-intensity training. Every student is treated like family, yet challenged with rigorous workouts, technical drills, and strategy-focused sessions. Training is continuous, with mandatory league matches and monthly events sponsored by the academy. Our specialized programs cater to school and university athletes, as well as those preparing for elite competitions, including customized coaching for differently-abled players. With a proven track record of success, including a Commonwealth medalist, we are committed to shaping champions. Our goal is to set a new standard in table tennis coaching, ensuring every player receives the best training possible. By fostering a culture of discipline and excellence, we aim to build the top table tennis academy in India, providing world-class mentorship and opportunities for aspiring athletes.
